CampusPay

One card. Every campus need.

campuspay.app

What if your student ID was the only thing you ever needed on campus?

CampusPay is an IoT-powered prototype that turns a regular RFID-enabled student ID into a universal campus payment tool, built end-to-end: physical RFID hardware, a Django backend, a live web dashboard, and machine learning models running quietly underneath.

Physical RFID Hardware Arduino UNO R4 WiFi Django Backend Live Web Dashboard Random Forest ML Isolation Forest Anomaly Detection

The Old Way

No cash. No UPI failures due to bad campus networks. No juggling separate cards or multiple apps. Student friction at every mess queue and store counter.

The CampusPay Way

Just tap to eat in the mess, grab a quick snack, buy stationery — even pay at a different campus's outlet, seamlessly linked on the platform.

What's Actually Working

Production-grade intelligence & architecture, not just a concept.

Seamless Cashless Payments

A student's everyday RFID-enabled ID becomes the universal payment tool. A dual-wallet system splits "Mess Credits" (meals) from "Campus Credits" (canteen, stationery, and more) — tap-to-eat in the mess, order-then-tap in stores, with cross-campus interoperability built into the architecture.

Smart Mess Management

A Random Forest Regressor predicts exactly how much food the mess needs to prepare each day, factoring in attendance patterns, exam schedules, festivals, and menu trends — cutting food waste at the source instead of guessing.

Privacy-First Wellbeing Monitoring

A passive anomaly detection layer (Isolation Forest + time-series analysis) watches transaction patterns — skipped meals, irregular timings, reduced activity — for early signs of burnout or isolation. It surfaces anonymized alerts to counselors, never raw student data, so support can be offered early without anyone being watched.
